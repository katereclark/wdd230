Membership Levels

Non-Profit ($100)

  • Support for small businesses
  • Free networking and social events each month
  • Listing in the membership directory
  • Marketing and sponsorship opportunities
  • Advocacy and business development opportunities

Bronze ($200)

  • Exclusive registration pricing to annual and seasonal events
  • Communication and updates on events, programs, and services
  • Business and customer referrals
  • Publicity and marketing opportunities

Silver ($400)

  • Free quarterly business education trainings and workshops
  • Membership plaques for your place of business
  • A vote in the Chamber Committee
  • Discount if you refer a friend

Gold ($1000)

  • A seat on the Chamber Council
  • Tickets to a luncheon with the Chamber President
  • Nationally recognized leadership development and young professionals programs
