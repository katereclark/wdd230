Site Purpose

The purpose of Solaris Weather & Activities is to provide a place for trip planners of all ages and abilities to get the information they need to have the fun experience they are hoping for. Planning an adventure can be difficult for anyone, not to mention those with disabilities. The outing you have been planning for months is finally at the doors and the sun is shining, but who knows if the sky will break open when you least expect it? Looking for something exciting to do but not sure where to begin? Solaris Weather & Activities brings you the latest weather reports, news, and activities for the Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven areas so you don't miss a beat.
